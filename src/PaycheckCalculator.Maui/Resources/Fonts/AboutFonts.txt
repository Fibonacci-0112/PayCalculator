Place TrueType/OpenType font files used by the MAUI shell here.

MauiProgram.cs registers `OpenSans-Regular.ttf` (alias `OpenSansRegular`) and `OpenSans-Semibold.ttf`
(alias `OpenSansSemibold`). Drop the matching `.ttf` files into this folder. They are bundled at build
time via the `<MauiFont Include="Resources\Fonts\*" />` glob in the csproj.

  https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans
